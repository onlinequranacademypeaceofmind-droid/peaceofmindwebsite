About Us

Our Story & Mission

Dedicated to spreading the light of the Holy Quran across the world

نُورٌ عَلَى نُورٍ

"Light upon Light" — Surah An-Nur 24:35

Peace of Mind Online Quran Academy is built on the belief that every Muslim deserves access to quality Quran education, no matter where they are in the world.

Who We Are

Spreading Quran Knowledge to Every Home

Peace of Mind Online Quran Academy is an online platform dedicated to teaching the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed. We bring quality Islamic education to students around the world from the comfort of their homes.

Our academy was founded with a simple yet powerful mission — to make Quran learning accessible, easy, and peaceful for every Muslim family, regardless of their location or schedule.

🎯 Our Mission: To spread Quran knowledge and build strong Islamic values in children and adults around the world.
